Tacos recipe

These delicious meal is perfect for taco Tuesday with the family or friends

  • 8 Taco Shells
  • 1 pound hamburger
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1 medium onion, chopped (about 1/2 cup)
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 cup of shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 large tomato, chopped
  • 1/2 cup dairy sour cream

Instructions

  1. Cook and stir hamburger in 10-inch skillet until light brown; drain
  2. Stir in water, '2 cup onion, the chili powder, salt, cumin and garlic
  3. Heat to boiling; reduce heat
  4. Simmer uncovered, stirring occasionally, until thickened, about 10 minutes
  5. Spoon about % cup hamburger mixture into each shell
  6. Top with shredded lettuce, chopped onion, shredded cheese, chopped tomato and sour cream
