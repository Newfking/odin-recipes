Sweet and Sour Meatballs recipe

Description

Who doesn't like sweet and sour, name says it all. Enjoy

Ingredents

  • 16 Meatballs
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 can (13'4 ounces) pineapple chunks
  • 1/3 cup vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 small green pepper, coarsely chopped

Instructions

  1. Cook Meatballs in skillet; remove
  2. Drain fat from skillet
  3. Mix brown sugar and cornstarch in skillet
  4. Stir in pineapple (with syrup), vinegar and soy sauce
  5. Heat to boiling, stirring constantly; reduce heat
  6. Add meatballs
  7. Cover; simmer, stirring occasionally, 10 minutes
  8. Stir in green pepper
  9. Cover; sim-mer until crisp-tender, 5 minutes
